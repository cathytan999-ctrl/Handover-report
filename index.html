Property Handover Entry Report

Capture photos, dictate condition notes into text, and generate a clean entry report for handover.

1. Handover Details

Enter the basic property and inspection information.

2. Rooms / Areas

Choose how many areas you want in this report.

3. Condition Capture

Take photos, speak condition notes, and tick observed items for each room.
Press Build Room Checklist to create your rooms.

4. Generate Report

Build a printable entry report from the details you captured.
Voice-to-text uses your browser’s speech recognition when available. On unsupported browsers, you can still record notes manually by typing into the room notes box.
